About Me

I trained as an English teacher, where I spent the first few years of my career. I ended up teaching coding and pioneering our school's first AP program. When COVID hit, I trained as a software developer. Those years in the tech industry led me to a proficiency in research and a curiosity for the unknown. I've since found my niche in technical writing.

I have over 12 years of experience as an exceptionally precise writer and editor, as well as over 5 years in the technology sector. My ability to synthesize and present abstract technical concepts, using tools like Word, PowerPoint, and Visio, as well as through curriculum creation and live presentations, sets me apart from other technical writers.

In addition, I independently manage and organize multiple documentation pipelines at once. I can quickly pivot and adapt as situations and priorities change.

My great people skills and approachable, analytical personality lend itself well to communicating with highly-technical individuals. I have a high value for all people, and that respect wins trust and helps create an enjoyable working environment for everyone.

My sharp eye for organization, as well as my ability to grasp and communicate a high level of subject-matter knowledge, make me an ideal technical writer. And when deadlines are looming, I am always calm.
